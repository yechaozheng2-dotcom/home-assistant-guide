## Why Zigbee2mqtt?

Zigbee is a low-power, mesh networking protocol that's perfect for smart homes. Unlike Wi-Fi, Zigbee devices can communicate directly with one another, enhancing efficiency and connectivity. Zigbee2mqtt acts as a bridge, allowing you to control Zigbee devices using MQTT, which is lightweight and works well for real-time applications. This makes Zigbee2mqtt a reliable choice for users with a Home Assistant setup, ensuring your smart devices work together seamlessly.
