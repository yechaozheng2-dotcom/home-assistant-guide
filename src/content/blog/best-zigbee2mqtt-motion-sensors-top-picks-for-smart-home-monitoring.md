### Detailed Overview of Our Top Picks

#### 1. Aqara Motion Sensor

The **Aqara Motion Sensor** is compact and reliable. It features a detection range of up to **7 meters** and has an adjustable sensitivity setting, allowing you to customize how sensitive it is to motion. You can find it for about **$20**, which makes it an affordable investment for your smart home. 

**Pros:**
- Long battery life (up to **2 years** with standard usage).
- Seamless integration with **Zigbee2MQTT** and popular home automation systems like Home Assistant.
- Compact design, ideal for discreet placement.

**Cons:**
- Limited range compared to some competitors, but still sufficient for most use cases.

If you're a DIY enthusiast looking to optimize your home automation system, the Aqara Motion Sensor is likely the best choice. 

#### 2. Philips Hue Motion Sensor

The **Philips Hue Motion Sensor** is well-known for its exceptional build quality. It performs well in various environments and sells for around **$40**. Besides being compatible with Zigbee2MQTT, it works with **HomeKit**, which is perfect if you use Apple's ecosystem.

**Pros:**
- Ambient light detection adjusts sensitivity based on available light, thus extending battery life.
- Sleek design, making it aesthetically pleasing for indoor use.

**Cons:**
- Higher price point, which may not be justified if you only need basic motion detection.

If you utilize multiple Philips Hue products or prefer HomeKit, this sensor will enhance your smart home experience.

#### 3. Sonoff PIR2

If you're looking for an extremely budget-friendly option, the **Sonoff PIR2** is available for around **$15**. It boasts an impressive detection range of **8 meters** and supports multiple states (e.g., ON/OFF) for flexible configurations. 

**Pros:**
- Affordable price makes it an excellent entry-level option.
- Tri-state output allows for more versatile automation setups.

**Cons:**
- Shorter battery life (around **3 years**) compared to higher-end models.
  
This sensor is ideal for those newcomers to smart home automation who need reliable motion detection without breaking the bank.

#### 4. MOSQUITO PIR Sensor

The **MOSQUITO PIR Sensor** comes at an enticing price of **$12**, making it the most affordable pick on this list. While it has a shorter battery life of 1 year, it's lightweight and easy to install, making it perfect for those just starting with smart home setups.

**Pros:**
- Very budget-friendly for basic motion detection needs.
- Lightweight and simple installation.

**Cons:**
- Limited battery life and functionality compared to competitors.

If you're only looking for basic motion detection in a non-critical area and are on a tight budget, this is a solid choice.
