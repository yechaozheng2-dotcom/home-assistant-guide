### Detailed Reviews of Top Picks

#### 1. ConBee II

The **ConBee II** is a standout hub that offers seamless integration with Zigbee2MQTT. Priced at around **$35**, it not only connects over **200 devices** but also boasts an impressive range of up to **100 meters** in open spaces. 

**Key Features:**
- **Compatibility:** Works with a wide variety of Zigbee devices, including popular brands like Philips Hue and IKEA Tradfri.
- **User-Friendly Experience:** The web-based user interface allows easy monitoring and management of connected devices.
- **Firmware Upgrades:** Regular updates ensure compatibility with the latest devices and improvements to performance.

*Who This is For:* If you are a smart home enthusiast who wants flexibility and control over a vast array of devices, the ConBee II is an exceptional choice. 

#### 2. Sonoff Zigbee 3.0 USB Dongle Plus

If you’re tightening your budget, the **Sonoff Zigbee 3.0 USB Dongle Plus** at approximately **$20** provides excellent value. Although it supports **up to 100 devices**, its straightforward installation process and compact design make it appealing for beginners.

**Key Features:**
- **Budget-Friendly:** Offers a cost-effective solution for users with fewer devices.
- **Compact Design:** Small USB dongle format makes it easy to integrate into your existing setup.
- **Good Range:** Generally effective range of **up to 50 meters**, which is adequate for small to medium homes.

*Who Should Skip This:* If your home has numerous Zigbee devices or you require a wider range, this dongle may not meet your needs.

#### 3. CC2652P Coordinator

At around **$25**, the **CC2652P Coordinator** is another robust choice. It’s known for stability and is compatible with over **200 devices**, making it an excellent alternative to the ConBee II.

**Key Features:**
- **Powerful Chip:** The CC2652P chip is widely regarded for its superior stability and performance.
- **Long Range:** Like the ConBee II, it boasts a range of up to **100 meters**, suitable for larger homes.
- **Open-Source:** This coordinator supports various custom firmware options, appealing to tech-savvy users.

*Who This is For:* If you're looking for a powerful hub that can support larger installations and many devices, the CC2652P Coordinator is your best bet.

#### 4. Zigbee2MQTT JS Coordinator

Priced at **$40**, the **Zigbee2MQTT JS Coordinator** offers unique features for advanced users. Its functionality can be tailored through a variety of settings.

**Key Features:**
- **Custom Integration:** Perfect for those who like to dive deep into settings.
- **Good Support:** Strong developer community for troubleshooting and enhancements.
- **Decent Compatibility:** Works well with many Zigbee devices, though it might require some tweaks.

*Who Should Skip This:* If you're a newcomer, the advanced options may be overwhelming and unnecessary, making simpler options preferable.

### Conclusion: What You Should Buy

After weighing the options, I recommend going with the **ConBee II** if you want the best overall performance and compatibility. However, if you're constrained by budget, the **Sonoff Zigbee 3.0 USB Dongle Plus** is an excellent starter option. 

In the world of smart home devices, having a reliable hub is crucial for smooth automation and control. Choose based on your needs, and enjoy the convenience and versatility that Zigbee2MQTT offers!
